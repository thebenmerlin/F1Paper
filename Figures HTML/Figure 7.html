Space Vector PWM (SVPWM) Voltage Vector Hexagon with Sector Identification — 3-Phase SiC Inverter, 648 V DC Bus. Six active vectors V₁–V₆ and two zero vectors V₀(000), V₇(111). Reference vector Vref synthesised by time-weighted combination of adjacent sector vectors.
SVPWM hexagon for 3-phase SiC inverter (648 V DC bus, fsw = 20 kHz). Active vectors V1–V6 have magnitude ⅔·Vdc and are separated by 60° in the αβ stationary frame. Zero vectors V0(000) and V7(111) lie at the origin. Reference vector Vref in Sector I is synthesised by time-weighting adjacent vectors: Vref·Ts = T1·V1 + T2·V2 + T0·V0,7, where T1 + T2 + T0 = Ts (switching period). Switching states shown as (SaSbSc) per phase leg.
