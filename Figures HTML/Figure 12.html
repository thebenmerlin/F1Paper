Suspension Geometry Diagram — Front and Rear Double Wishbone with Pullrod Actuation. Kinematic instant centres (IC) shown via extended construction lines. Inboard rocker and spring–damper assemblies mounted to monocoque (front) and gearbox bell-housing (rear).
Front and rear double wishbone geometry with pullrod actuation (both axles). Inboard spring–damper units mounted to monocoque (front) and gearbox bell-housing (rear). Instant centres (IC) located far outboard of wheel plane — consistent with high anti-dive (front) and anti-squat (rear) geometry for aerodynamic ride height sensitivity. Rocker amplifies pullrod motion to damper stroke. Inerter (not shown) connected in parallel with spring–damper unit at rocker. Camber: front −3.2°, rear −1.8°. Toe: front 0.05° out, rear 0.15° in. Caster angle: 5.5° (front).
