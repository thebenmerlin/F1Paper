Electronics Architecture Block Diagram — Network Domains, ECU, BMS, SiC Inverter, Sensors, Telemetry, and Power Distribution Paths. CAN FD backbone at 5 Mbit/s. HV bus: 648 V / 540 A. LV bus: 12 V auxiliary.
CAN FD backbone at 5 Mbit/s connects all control and sensor nodes with deterministic latency. Main ECU (FPGA + ARM Cortex-A) hosts FOC algorithm, SOC-tiered state machine, and energy management. BMS monitors all 360 cells individually: voltage, temperature, SOC (Coulomb counting + Kalman filter), SOH. HV interlock and fusing managed by PDU; LV auxiliary bus powers ECU, sensors, and telemetry. Resolver provides θr, ωr at 10 000 counts/rev for FOC feedback. Telemetry transmits 100+ channels at 100 Hz to pit wall in real time.
