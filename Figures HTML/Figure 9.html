Figure 9
Integrated Thermal Management Architecture — Four Independent Cooling Loops and Sidepod Packaging. Total peak rejection: 454 kW. All loops isolated; no cross-contamination. Sidepod core houses Loops 1 and 3.
All four loops are hydraulically isolated; no cross-contamination between circuits. Loops 1 and 3 share the primary and secondary sidepod cores respectively. Loop 2 operates at lower temperature to maintain NMC cell temperature below 45°C. Loop 4 oil-spray cools MGU-K stator and rotor; direct liquid plate cools SiC MOSFET baseplate (Tj,max = 175°C). Peak total rejection 454 kW; average 383 kW. ICE exhaust accounts for remaining ~150 kW not captured in coolant loops.
