Field Oriented Control (FOC) Block Diagram — MGU-K PMSM Drive System (2026 F1 Hybrid). Clarke and Park coordinate transforms, PI current regulators (d/q axes), SVPWM synthesis, SiC inverter switching, and rotor resolver feedback. DC bus: 648 V; peak power: 350 kW.
FOC implementation for MGU-K PMSM drive. MTPA sets id* = 0 (non-salient rotor). Clarke: (abc) → (αβ) stationary frame. Park: (αβ) → (dq) synchronous frame aligned to θr. Inverse Park: (dq) → (αβ) voltage references. SVPWM synthesises switching signals Sa, Sb, Sc (fsw = 20 kHz). Resolver supplies θr and ωr to both Park and Park⁻¹ blocks.
