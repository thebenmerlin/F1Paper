MGU-K Output Torque vs. ICE Torque Curve — Low-RPM Torque Fill Strategy. MGU-K delivers constant 222 Nm from 0 RPM, compensating for turbo lag below 4 000 RPM. Combined peak: 644 Nm at 8 000 RPM. ICE peak: 422 Nm at 8 000 RPM. Field weakening onset: 14 500 RPM.
Torque fill strategy: MGU-K delivers constant 222 Nm from standstill, compensating for ICE turbo lag below 4 000 RPM where turbocharged output is insufficient. ICE torque rises steeply 2 000–6 000 RPM as boost builds; peak 422 Nm at 8 000 RPM. Combined system peak 644 Nm at 8 000 RPM. MGU-K field weakening onset at 14 500 RPM. Torque values derived from rated power: PICE = 450 kW, PMGU-K = 350 kW. MGU-K constant-torque region: T = P / ω limited by peak current Ipk = 540 A, Kt = 0.41 Nm/A.
